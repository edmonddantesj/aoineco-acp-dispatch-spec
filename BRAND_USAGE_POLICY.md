# Brand Usage Policy — ACP Dispatch / ACP 항해일지 (v0.1)

This repository’s templates and formats are MIT-licensed to maximize reuse.

However, the **names and marks** below are brand identifiers. This policy exists to prevent confusion or impersonation.

## Covered brand identifiers
- **ACP Dispatch** (EN series name)
- **ACP 항해일지** (KR series name)
- **Aoineco & Co.** (publisher identifier)

## Allowed
- Reuse, modify, and republish the **format/templates** under the MIT license.
- Reference the origin in an attribution line (recommended).
- Use phrases like:
  - “Based on the ACP Dispatch format (MIT)”
  - “Derived from ACP Dispatch templates”

## Not allowed (without explicit permission)
- Representing your fork or publication as an **official** ACP Dispatch / ACP 항해일지 release.
- Using **Aoineco & Co.** branding in a way that implies endorsement or authorship.
- Using “Official” or confusingly similar naming that suggests you are the source publisher.

## Guidance for forks
If you fork the format:
- Rename your series (recommended)
- Keep an attribution line to the original format
- Make it clear what you changed

## Contact
If you want to publish an official collaboration or co-branded edition, contact the maintainers via the repository issues.
